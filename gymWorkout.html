Chest

🟢 Beginner

Machine Chest Press

Target: Pectoralis major (lower/mid)

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15 | Rest: 60 sec

Sit upright on the machine, grip the handles at chest height, press forward until arms are fully extended. Slowly return.

Equipment Needed: Chest press machine

Incline Dumbbell Press

Target: Upper chest

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 | Rest: 90 sec

Set bench to 30-45°. Press dumbbells from chest level upward in a slight arc.

Equipment Needed: Adjustable bench, dumbbells

🟡 Intermediate

Barbell Bench Press

Target: Pectoralis major, anterior deltoid, triceps

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 90 sec

Lie flat on bench, un-rack bar, lower to mid-chest, press up explosively.

Equipment Needed: Barbell, bench, safety rack

Cable Chest Fly

Target: Pectoralis minor, chest inner fibres

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 60 sec

Stand between two cable stations, bring hands together in a wide arc at chest height.

Equipment Needed: Cable machine with D-handles

🔴 Advanced

Weighted Dips

Target: Lower chest, triceps

Sets: 4 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 2 min

Attach a weight belt. Lean slightly forward on the parallel bars for chest emphasis, lower until upper arms are parallel to floor.

Equipment Needed: Dip bars, weight belt

Decline Barbell Press

Target: Lower chest

Sets: 4 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 2 min

Secure feet on decline bench. Lower the bar to the lower chest and press powerfully.

Equipment Needed: Decline bench, barbell, safety rack

Back

🟢 Beginner

Seated Cable Row

Target: Latissimus dorsi, rhomboids

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15 | Rest: 60 sec

Sit at the cable row machine, pull the handle to your lower abdomen, keeping elbows close to torso.

Equipment Needed: Cable row machine

Lat Pulldown

Target: Latissimus dorsi

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 60 sec

Grip bar wider than shoulder-width. Pull down to upper chest while leaning back slightly.

Equipment Needed: Lat pulldown machine

🟡 Intermediate

Barbell Bent-Over Row

Target: Lats, traps, rear deltoids

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 90 sec

Hinge at hips ~45°, pull barbell to lower ribs, squeeze shoulder blades at top.

Equipment Needed: Barbell, weight plates

🔴 Advanced

Weighted Pull-Ups

Target: Lats, biceps, core

Sets: 4 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 2 min

Hang from pull-up bar with added weight. Pull chest to bar, controlling the descent.

Equipment Needed: Pull-up bar, weight belt

🦵 Legs

🟢 Beginner

Leg Press

Target: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 | Rest: 60 sec

Sit in machine, place feet shoulder-width on platform, push until knees are nearly straight.

Equipment Needed: Leg press machine

Seated Leg Curl

Target: Hamstrings

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 60 sec

Curl the pad toward glutes against resistance.

Equipment Needed: Leg curl machine

🟡 Intermediate

Barbell Back Squat

Target: Quads, glutes, hamstrings, core

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 2 min

Bar rests on upper traps. Squat to at least parallel, drive through heels to stand.

Equipment Needed: Squat rack, barbell, weight plates

🔴 Advanced

Romanian Deadlift (Heavy)

Target: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back

Sets: 4 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 2 min

Maintain a slight knee bend, hinge at hips until bar is just below knees, return by squeezing glutes.

Equipment Needed: Barbell, weight plates

Shoulders

🟢 Beginner

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Target: Medial deltoid

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 | Rest: 45 sec

Hold dumbbells at sides, raise arms to shoulder height, lower slowly.

Equipment Needed: Dumbbells

🟡 Intermediate

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Target: Anterior and medial deltoids, triceps

Sets: 4 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 90 sec

Press dumbbells overhead from ear height, keep core braced.

Equipment Needed: Adjustable bench, dumbbells

🔴 Advanced

Barbell Overhead Press (Standing)

Target: Full deltoid complex, upper traps, triceps

Sets: 4 | Reps: 5-6 | Rest: 2 min

Press bar from front rack position to lockout overhead. Control descent.

Equipment Needed: Barbell, weight plates, squat rack

💪 Arms (Biceps & Triceps)

🟢 Beginner

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

Target: Biceps brachii

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 60 sec

Curl both dumbbells alternately to shoulder height, keep elbows pinned.

Equipment Needed: Dumbbells

Tricep Pushdown (Cable)

Target: Triceps

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 | Rest: 60 sec

Using a rope attachment, push down until elbows are fully extended.

Equipment Needed: Cable machine, rope attachment

🟡 Intermediate

Barbell Curl

Target: Biceps brachii, brachialis

Sets: 4 | Reps: 8-10 | Rest: 60 sec

Use straight or EZ bar. Curl from full extension to full contraction without swinging.

Equipment Needed: Barbell or EZ bar

🔴 Advanced

Close-Grip Bench Press

Target: Triceps, anterior deltoid

Sets: 4 | Reps: 6-8 | Rest: 90 sec

Grip bar shoulder-width. Lower to chest, elbows tracking inward, press up.

Equipment Needed: Barbell, bench, safety rack

Core & Abs

🟢 Beginner

Plank

Target: Transverse abdominis, obliques, lower back

Sets: 3 | Duration: 30 sec | Rest: 30 sec

Maintain a straight line from head to heels on forearms and toes.

Equipment Needed: Mat

🔴 Advanced

Hanging Leg Raise

Target: Lower abs, hip flexors

Sets: 4 | Reps: 12-15 | Rest: 60 sec

Hang from pull-up bar. Raise straight legs to 90° (or higher) controlling momentum.

Equipment Needed: Pull-up bar
