🪑 Minimal Equipment Routines

Workouts using one or more of: resistance bands, dumbbells, a chair, or a yoga mat.

Resistance Band Full-Body Circuit

Equipment: Resistance band | Duration: 30 minutes

1. Banded Squat

Sets: 3 | Reps: 15 | Rest: 45 sec

Stand on band, hold ends at shoulders, perform a squat under the resistance.

2. Banded Row

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 45 sec

Anchor band at waist height, pull both hands toward hips squeezing shoulder blades.

3. Banded Lateral Walk

Sets: 3 | Duration: 45 sec per direction | Rest: 30 sec

Band around ankles, step sideways maintaining tension. Targets glutes and abductors.
