👴 Senior-Friendly Home Workouts

Low-impact, joint-friendly routines designed to improve balance, posture, and daily mobility for users aged 60 and above.

Chair Yoga Flow

Duration: 20 minutes | Intensity: Very Low | Equipment: Chair

1. Seated Cat-Cow

Reps: 10 cycles

Sit upright at chair edge. Arch and round the spine alternately, following the breath.

2. Seated Leg Raise

Sets: 2 | Reps: 10 per leg

Seated in chair, extend one leg straight and hold for 3 sec, lower slowly.

3. Heel & Toe Raises

Sets: 2 | Reps: 15

Seated, alternate raising heels and toes. Improves ankle mobility and circulation.
