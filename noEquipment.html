🚫 No Equipment Routines

These exercises use only your bodyweight - perfect for any space.

📉 Weight Loss Circuit

Duration: 30 minutes | Intensity: Moderate-High | Frequency: 4-5 days/week

1. Jumping Jacks

Sets: 3 | Duration: 60 seconds | Rest: 15 sec

Keep feet together, jump to spread legs wide while raising arms overhead. Return to start.

2. Burpees

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 30 sec

Drop into a squat, kick legs back to plank, perform one push-up, jump feet forward, leap up with hands overhead.

3. High Knees

Sets: 3 | Duration: 45 sec | Rest: 15 sec

Run in place, driving knees up to hip height alternately.

4. Mountain Climbers

Sets: 3 | Duration: 45 sec | Rest: 30 sec

In a high plank, drive knees alternately toward the chest in a running motion.

💪 Bodyweight Muscle Building

Duration: 45 minutes | Intensity: Moderate | Frequency: 3-4 days/week

1. Push-Ups

Sets: 4 | Reps: 15-20 | Rest: 60 sec

Standard push-up keeping body straight. Vary hand position (wide/narrow) for different targets.

2. Bodyweight Squats

Sets: 4 | Reps: 20 | Rest: 60 sec

Feet shoulder-width apart, lower until thighs are parallel to the floor, drive up through heels.

3. Tricep Dips (Chair)

Sets: 3 | Reps: 12-15 | Rest: 60 sec

Hands on edge of a stable chair, lower body by bending elbows to 90°, press back up.

Equipment: Stable chair

4. Glute Bridge

Sets: 3 | Reps: 20 | Rest: 45 sec

Lie on back, feet flat, thrust hips to ceiling squeezing glutes at the top.

5. Pike Push-Up

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10-12 | Rest: 60 sec

Inverted-V position, lower head toward floor by bending elbows. Targets shoulders.

🧘 Flexibility & Mobility Flow

Duration: 20-30 minutes | Intensity: Low | Frequency: Daily

1. Cat-Cow Stretch

Sets: 2 | Reps: 10 cycles | Rest: None

On hands and knees, alternate arching the back (cow) and rounding it (cat).

2. World's Greatest Stretch

Sets: 2 | Reps: 5 per side | Rest: None

Lunge forward, rotate upper body toward lead leg, reach overhead. Targets hips, thoracic spine.

3. Hip Flexor Stretch (Kneeling)

Duration: 30 sec per side | Sets: 2

Lunge with one knee on floor. Shift weight forward to feel stretch in front hip.

4. Doorway Chest Stretch

Duration: 30 sec per side | Sets: 2

Place forearm on doorframe at shoulder height, rotate away to open the chest.

🏃 Endurance Training

Duration: 30-60 minutes | Intensity: Moderate | Frequency: 3-5 days/week

1. Jump Rope (Simulated)

Sets: 5 | Duration: 2 min | Rest: 30 sec

Mimic jump-rope motion without a rope: small jumps on the balls of your feet while swinging arms.

2. Step-Ups

Sets: 4 | Reps: 15 per leg | Rest: 45 sec

Use a sturdy step or stair. Step up, step down, alternating lead legs.

Equipment: Step or stair

3. Squat Jumps

Sets: 4 | Reps: 12 | Rest: 60 sec

Squat down, explode upward into a jump, land softly back into the squat position.
