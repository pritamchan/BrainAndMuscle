👦 Kids & Teens Fitness (Ages 6-17)

Fun, playful, and safe exercises that build strength, coordination, and healthy habits in young people.

Fun Movement Circuit

Duration: 20 minutes | Intensity: Moderate | Frequency: Daily

1. Animal Walks

Duration: 5 min total

Bear crawl (30 sec), crab walk (30 sec), frog jumps (30 sec). Builds coordination and full-body strength in a playful way.

2. Freeze Dance

Duration: 5 min

Dance freely to music, freeze when it stops. Improves cardio and listening skills.

3. Obstacle Course

Duration: 10 min

Use household items (pillows, cushions, books) to build a mini obstacle course. Encourages creativity and agility.
